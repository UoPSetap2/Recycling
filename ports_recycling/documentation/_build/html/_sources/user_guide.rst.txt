User Guide
==========

This section provides a detailed guide on how to use the Portsmouth Recycling App.

Browsing the Map for Recycling Points
--------------------------------------

1. Open the Portsmouth Recycling App.
2. Tap on the "Map" option in the main menu.
3. The app will display a map with the recycling points near your location.
4. Tap on any recycling point to see what can be recycled there.

Searching for Recyclable Materials
----------------------------------

1. Open the Portsmouth Recycling App.
2. Tap on the "Search" option in the main menu.
3. Enter the material you want to recycle in the search bar.
4. The app will display information about how to dispose of the material and where it can be recycled.

Checking Bin Collection Dates
-----------------------------

1. Open the Portsmouth Recycling App.
2. Tap on the "Bin Collection Dates" option in the main menu.
3. The app will display the bin collection dates for your home address.

Remember to update your home address in the app settings if you move to a new location.
